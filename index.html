What's my Name Again?

I am adventurous and adapatble, which reminded my friends of the elusive main character of the video game, Where in the World is Carmen Sandiego. Although I am not a thief like Carmen is in the game, I do make it my mission to acquire precious knowledge and experience wherever I go.
