What's My Name Again?

My name Agata is a traditional Polish name. It is usually mispronounced as Agatha by most people. To my friends, it seemed like an old-fashioned name that did not suit who I am. I am adventurous and adaptable, which reminded my friends of the elusive main character of the video game, Where in the World is Carmen Sandiego. Besides the folks that sign my paychecks, most people on Oahu do not know my real name! Although I am not a thief as Carmen is in the game, I do make it my mission to acquire precious knowledge and experience wherever I go.
