What's my Name Again?

My name Agata is a traditional Polish name. It is usually mispronounced as Agatha by most people. To my friends, it seemed like an old-fashioned name that did not suit who I am. I am adventurous and adapatble, which reminded my friends of the elusive main character of the video game, Where in the World is Carmen Sandiego. After moving to Hawaii with a friend for college, she would only introduced me as Carmen. Besides the folks that sign my paychecks, most people on Oahu do not know my real name. Although I am not a thief like Carmen is in the game, I do make it my mission to acquire precious knowledge and experience wherever I go.
