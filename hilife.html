How did I get to Oahu?

I received a scholarship to Hawaii Pacific University and moved there in 2010. This move changed my life forever. Living as a college student in one of the most expensive cities in the US taught me how to manage my money and live simply.

Aloha Spirit

The term Aloha is complicated to define. Simply, it is a greeting or a farewell but, experiencing Aloha is something special. Embracing and being embraced by the island, ocean, culture and people brings an overwhelming feeling of love and acceptance. That is the best way I can explain my experience with the Aloha Spirit.

My Favorite Island Activity: Hiking

From high ridgelines you can see only the ocean surrounds this perspective shows just how isolated the islands are; to getting lost in the rainforest and chasing waterfalls, there are many hikes to witness the beauty of the islands.

    Below are a few of my favorite hikes on Oahu!

  1. Ka'au Crater
  2. Lani Kai Pillbox
  3. Koko Head
  4. Haiku Stairs
CAUTION

If you were planning on hiking Haiku Stairs, aka "Stairway To Heaven", please know that it is ILLEGAL. You can be fined for trespassing. Also, the stairs have not been repaired since 2003. This hike is not maintained therefore, it is extremely DANGEROUS.
