Settle in Seattle

After much thought, I decided to leave my beloved island of Oahu. I first backpacked through Europe and then decided to try to settle down somewhere on the Mainland (contiguous United States). My first stop was Houston, Texas and it deemed to be too crowded and humid for me. The next stop was northern Colorado. It was the complete opposite of my experience in Houston. I lived on a horse ranch in a remote town, and it was cold and dry. Although there were many opportunities to enjoy two of my favorite hobbies, hiking and drinking local craft beer, my body made me very aware that it did not want to acclimate to Colorado's high altitude and dry climate. In result, I left Colorado and drove to Seattle.

Reasons for Relocation:

  • Career Change. My goal is to find a career in computer programming. I am optimistic that I can provide a great contribution to the field after completing Epicodus.
  • Climate and Georgraphy. From the mountains to the sea Washington provides an ideal living environment for me. The summers here are a treasured secret and provide many opportunities for hiking and enjoying nature.
  • Craft Beer. A dream of mine is to one day brew my own beer. Until then, Seattle provides many lovely breweries to conduct research until I am able to brew my own.
