Pacific Northwest Paradise

Settle in Seattle

After much thought and travel, I decided to move away from my beloved island of Oahu and give the Mainland a try. My first stop was Houston, Texas and it deemed to be too crowded and humid for me. The next stop was northern Colorado. It was the complete opposite of my experience in Houston. I lived on a horse ranch in a remote town, and it was cold and dry. Although there were many opportunities to enjoy two of my favorite hobbies, hiking and drinking local craft beer, my body made me very aware that it did not want to acclimate to Colorado's high altitude and dry climate. In result, I left Colorado and drove to Seattle.

Reasons for Relocation:

  • Career Change. My goal is to find a career in the technology industry. Seattle has been said to be one of the nation's fastest growing tech hubs and I am optimistic that I can be a contribution to the field after completing Epicodus.
  • Climate and Georgraphy. Although the winters can be quite gray, snow fall and frigid tempatures are mild in comparison to Colorado or Chicago. The summers here are a treasured secret and provide many opportunities for hiking and enjoying nature.
  • Craft Beer. A dream of mine is to one day brew my own beer. Until then, Seattle provides many lovely breweries to do research.
